Inside the box, you find two PEOPLE. One of them introduces themselves as BIG JIM, while presenting his sole possession, a standard KEY. The other declares: "I AM LORD UNDERSHIRT, WIZARD ALMIGHTY. SET ME FREE, AND RECEIVE THIS VERY RARE SCROLL OF SCROLLING."

It almost appears as if you must now make a choice. The KEY? Or the VERY RARE SCROLL OF SCROLLING? After much thought, you decide to take both of them. The people do nothing, as they are small and malnourished from being inside of a box.

You obtain the KEY and the SCROLL OF SCROLLING.
